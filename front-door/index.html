Cabalmail · a disposable subdomain for every door

An address for every door.

Cabalmail turns the domain you already own into a wall of disposable subdomains. Hand your bank chase.yourname.com, your friends friends.yourname.com, every newsletter sign-up its own. When one starts leaking spam, you yank the subdomain from DNS — and the channel is shut for good.

One subdomain per correspondent.

Bank, employer, every form you fill out — each gets its own subdomain on yourname.com. When one goes bad, pull the DNS record and the address vanishes. No unsubscribe theater.

Built for the keyboard.

Read, archive, reply without leaving the home row. jk to move, e to archive, r to reply, ⌘K to find anything.

Quiet by design.

No trackers, no read receipts, no algorithmic re-ordering. Remote images stay blocked until you say otherwise. The inbox is a place to read, not be measured.
